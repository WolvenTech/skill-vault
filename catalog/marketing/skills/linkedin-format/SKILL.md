# LinkedIn Format

Structure and format rules for LinkedIn posts. Pair with brand voice skills before drafting.

## Post structure

1. **Hook** (1-2 lines) — Specific insight, contrarian take, or numbered promise.
2. **Context** — Why this matters now.
3. **Body** — 3-5 short paragraphs or a numbered list.
4. **Takeaway** — One clear lesson.
5. **CTA** — Optional; prefer soft invites (comment, follow) over hard sells.

## Formatting rules

- Line breaks every 1-2 sentences for mobile scanability.
- Use numbered lists for frameworks; bullets for examples.
- Limit hashtags to 3 relevant tags at the end.
- First comment can hold links; keep post body link-light.

## Cadence

- 2-4 posts per week for founder-led accounts.
- Alternate educational, story, and proof posts.
